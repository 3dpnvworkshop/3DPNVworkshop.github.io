About the workshop

Where 3D perception meets navigation

This workshop brings together researchers from the Hi! PARIS community working on 3D perception and robotics navigation. The program sits at the intersection of robotics, computer vision and photogrammetry — pairing keynote perspectives with talks from PhD students and early-career researchers across partner labs.
