Posters

Call for Posters

The poster session is the heart of 3DPNV — an informal venue for PhD students, postdocs and researchers to share work in progress on 3D perception, navigation, computer vision and photogrammetry, and to spark cross-lab collaborations within the Hi! PARIS community.

Who can present

PhD students, postdocs and researchers from Hi! PARIS partner institutions and collaborating labs. Early-stage and work-in-progress contributions are very welcome.

Topics

  • 3D perception & reconstruction
  • Robotics navigation & SLAM
  • LiDAR and point-cloud processing
  • Photogrammetry
  • Scene understanding & mapping

How it runs

A short poster-teaser slot (one minute per poster) opens the session in the morning, followed by the full poster session over the extended lunch break (12:20–14:00).

Format

Recommended poster size A0, portrait. Boards and fixings are provided on site. Please arrive early to mount your poster before the teaser slot.

How to submit

To propose a poster, send a title, author list and a short abstract (up to 150 words) to the organizers. Submission details and the deadline will be announced here — please check back soon.
